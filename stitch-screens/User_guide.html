01 // THE ORIGIN

THE LEGEND OF HAND-CRICKET

Born in the concrete playgrounds and school corridors of South Asia, Hand-Cricket was the ultimate equalizer. No bat, no ball—just pure cognitive warfare.

Hand-Cricket Pro evolves this nostalgic rivalry into a high-octane digital arena. Using advanced Computer Vision, your physical gestures are translated into virtual strikes, bringing the playground to the global stage.

02 // THE PROTOCOL

THE RULES OF THE GAME

01
psychology

The Duel

Both players (or Player vs CPU) simultaneously choose a number between 1 and 6 using finger gestures.

02
trending_up

Scoring

If the numbers differ, the Batter adds their chosen number to their total score. Timing is critical.

03
sports_cricket

The Out

If the numbers match exactly, the Batter is OUT. Roles switch immediately. Highest total wins.
